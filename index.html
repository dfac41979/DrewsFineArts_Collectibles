About DFAC

Drew’s Fine Arts & Collectibles Certification and Authentication Committee (DFAC) provides formal, institutional-grade authentication services for fine arts, collectibles, and cultural objects.

Every certificate is digitally logged, immutable, and contributes to provenance integrity across the global collecting community.
